the Philippines

"the beauty of the Philippines lies not just the surface, but the people who are a part of its origins. This is what makes us, a Filipino."

The Philippines is a Southeast Asian country; an archipelago which consists of about 7,641 islands. It is a place with diverse and rich cultures. It is mainly divided into three sections: Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. The varying islands results in a diverse collection of languages that are mutually unintelligible to each other
Luzon is located at the northern part of the Philippines; one of the three sections of the Philippines. Ethnic groups like the Aeta, Bolinao, Igorot, Gaddang live in the Northern sections of Luzon whilst other groups such as the Bicolanos and Batangas live in the southern parts of Luzon.
The Visayas is located at the central part of the Philippines. Within the family of Bisayan Languages, the most spoken language out of all in the group is Cebuano. This language has over 21 million speakers. Whilst Cebuano is a language by itself, many confuse other Bisayan Languages to be a variation or dialect of Cebuano, which is not the case.
Mindanao is located at the southern part of the Philippines. Being the second largest island in the Philippines, groups of people from the Mindanao have distinct beliefs varying from denominations of Christianity to the Islamic Religion. People were also exposed to the Hindu and Buddhist beliefs from the people of Indonesia and Malaysia.
